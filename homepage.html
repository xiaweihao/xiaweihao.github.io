Weihao Xia

I am currently a computer vision algorithm engineer at AR/VR Engineering Department, 2012 Labs, Huawei.

During 07/2019 to 10/2019, I was a research assistant at Tsinghua University, working with my master advisor Prof. Yujiu Yang in both Intelligent Computing Team and Shenzhen Key Laboratory of Information Science and Technology.

I'm interested in computer vision, machine learning, optimization, and image processing. Much of my research is about inferring the physical world (shape, depth, motion, paint, light, colors, etc) from images. My current research directions focus on image translation, image restoration and enhancement, no-reference image quality assessment and domain adaption.

Currently, I am looking for a Ph.D. position. If you find my work matching with your research area and intend to provide me a potential Ph.D. position, please contact me and let's make a difference!
