Biography

I am currently a computer vision algorithm engineer at AR/VR Engineering Department, 2012 Labs, Huawei.

During 07/2019 to 10/2019, I was a research assistant at Tsinghua University, working with my master advisor Prof. Yujiu Yang in both Intelligent Computing Team and Shenzhen Key Laboratory of Information Science and Technology.

I received my master degree in Control Engineering from School of Information Science and Technology, Tsinghua University (THU) in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Yujiu Yang. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from School of Information Science and Technology in Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU) from 2012 to 2016.

I'm interested in computer vision, machine learning, image and video processing. Much of my research is about image translation, restoration and enhancement, no-reference image quality assessment and domain adaption. My current research directions focus on inferring the physical world (shape, depth, motion, paint, light, colors, etc) from images. Specifically, I tend to develope image transltion method for Learning 3D Representations from Natural Images.
